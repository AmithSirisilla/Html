Japan spans over 6852 islands and is 377,975 sq km. Is also called as Nihonkoku.

Japan is the eleventh most populous country in the world, as well as one of the most densely populated and urbanised. There are about 47 administrative prefectures and eight traditional regions. It's capital city is TOKYO.
